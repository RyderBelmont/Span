How to play with Rinkah's alternate Halloween outfit

Step 1: Add the following line to Rinkah's meta.xml and behaviour.xml files, under <tags>:

        <tag>rinkah_halloween</tag>
		
Step 2: Move all files from the "rinkah_halloween" folder (within the "alternate_opponents" folder) to the "rinkah" folder (within the "opponents" folder)
